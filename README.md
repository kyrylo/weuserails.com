# We Use Rails

[We Use Rails](https://weuserails.com) is a curated list of Ruby on Rails web
apps. We help people curious about Rails discover real-world applications and
businesses built with Rails.

## Rails powers the world's best web apps

"From HELLO WORLD to IPO" isn’t just a catchy slogan — it’s a reality for
countless companies that have used Ruby on Rails to build and scale their
products. Since its debut in 2004, Rails has become a favorite among developers
for its simplicity, productivity, and powerful framework.

When people ask, “Who uses Rails?”, the usual examples are industry giants like
Basecamp, GitHub, and Shopify. But they’re just the tip of the iceberg. Rails
powers thousands of applications across a diverse range of businesses — from
scrappy startups to massive enterprises.

We Use Rails showcases a growing list of businesses and projects powered by Ruby
on Rails. Whether you’re a developer seeking inspiration or a business exploring
frameworks for your next project, our platform highlights the versatility and
success of Rails. Discover who’s using Rails and learn how it can power your
next big idea.
